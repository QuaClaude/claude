Track Offers

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM)

Ignite your curiosity and innovation with the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) program! This comprehensive curriculum prepares you for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world. Dive deep into scientific inquiry, technological advancements, engineering principles, and mathematical problem-solving. With hands-on projects, collaborative learning, and exposure to cutting-edge research, the STEM program equips you with critical skills for a wide range of exciting careers. Join us to explore, discover, and shape the future through STEM!

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

Discover the power of human interaction and society with the Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) program! This enriching curriculum delves into history, culture, politics, psychology, and more, fostering a deep understanding of human behavior and societal structures. Through critical thinking, research, and communication, you'll gain insights that prepare you for careers in education, law, journalism, social work, and beyond. Join the HUMSS program and become a thoughtful leader and advocate in shaping a better world!

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

Unlock your entrepreneurial potential with the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) program! This specialized track equips you with essential knowledge in accounting, finance, marketing, and business management. Through practical projects, case studies, and expert guidance, you'll develop the skills to excel in the business world. Whether you aspire to be an entrepreneur, financial analyst, or corporate leader, the ABM program provides the foundation for a successful and dynamic career. Join us and start building your path to business excellence!

Technical-Vocational adn Livelihood track

Empower your future with the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) track! Specialize in Automotive Servicing, Computer Systems Servicing, Electrical Installation and Maintenance, or Shielded Metal Arc Welding. This hands-on program equips you with practical skills and industry certifications, ensuring you're job-ready upon graduation. Join the TVL track and pave the way to a successful and rewarding professional journey!
